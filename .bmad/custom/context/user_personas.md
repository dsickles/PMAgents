# Primary Personas

## 1. Alex (The Student)
*   **Role**: Undergraduate Sophomore, undecided major.
*   **Goals**: Graduate on time, minimize debt, find a career path.
*   **Frustrations**: Confusing degree requirements, clunky registration interfaces, fear of taking the wrong class.
*   **Quote**: "I just want to know what I need to take so I can graduate."

## 2. Sarah (The Academic Advisor)
*   **Role**: Professional Advisor, caseload of 350 students.
*   **Goals**: Identify at-risk students early, maximize student retention, efficient meeting prep.
*   **Frustrations**: Disconnected data (notes in one system, grades in another), manual degree audits, lack of time.
*   **Quote**: "I spend 20 minutes prepping for a 30-minute appointment. I need that data at my fingertips."

## 3. Robert (The Registrar)
*   **Role**: University Registrar.
*   **Goals**: Ensure academic integrity, enforce policy, accurate reporting.
*   **Frustrations**: Exceptions, manual overrides, changing curriculum policies.
*   **Quote**: "If the system allows an invalid registration, it's a compliance nightmare."

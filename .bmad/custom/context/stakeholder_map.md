# Stakeholder Map

## Key Decision Makers
*   **VP of Student Product**: Owns the P&L and overall roadmap. Focus: Market share, retention.
*   **Director of Academic Services**: Direct report. Focus: Feature completeness vs. Ellucian/Oracle.

## Influencers
*   **Design Partner Group (DPG)**: A group of 5 universities (Ohio State, UBC, etc.) who validate early designs.
*   **Customer Success Managers (CSM)**: They hear the daily complaints.
*   **Sales Engineering**: They know what features are losing deals.

## The "Blockers" (Compliance/Legal)
*   **Security Architect**: Will block anything that risks PII exposure.
*   **Accessibility Lead**: Will block any UI that isn't keyboard navigable.

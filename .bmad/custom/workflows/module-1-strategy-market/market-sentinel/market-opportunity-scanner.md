# Market Opportunity Scanner

## Goal
To find "white space" opportunities where customer pain exists but solutions are inadequate.

## Steps
1.  **Scan**: Read analyst reports (Gartner, Forrester), user forums (Reddit, HackerNews), and review sites (G2, Capterra).
2.  **Cluster**: Group complaints and feature requests into themes using keyword analysis.
3.  **Rank**: Sort themes by frequency and intensity of pain.
4.  **Size the Prize**: Estimate TAM (Total Addressable Market) for each opportunity using analyst data or LinkedIn Sales Navigator.
5.  **Moat Assessment**: Rate each opportunity on "Defensibility" (Easy/Medium/Hard to copy).
6.  **Validation Plan**: Propose 3 customers to interview to confirm the pain is real.
7.  **Regulatory/Trend Drivers**: Identify external forces (regulations, tech shifts) creating the opportunity.

## Output (White Space Report)
Must include:
1.  **Top 3 Opportunities**: Ranked by market size and pain intensity.
2.  **Evidence**: Quotes from real users, analyst commentary, or review snippets.

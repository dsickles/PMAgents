# B2B PMF Health Check

## Goal
To assess whether we have achieved Product-Market Fit in the B2B context using enterprise-grade metrics.

## Steps
1.  **Logo Retention**: Calculate % of customers who renew contracts (target: >90%).
2.  **Net Revenue Retention (NRR)**: Include expansion revenue. Target: >110%.
3.  **Time to Value (TTV)**: Measure days from contract signature to first meaningful outcome (target: <90 days).
4.  **Champion Dependency**: Assess if we're dependent on one person at each account (risk indicator).
5.  **Expansion Rate**: Calculate % of customers who upgrade or buy add-ons (target: >30%).
6.  **Segment Analysis**: Break down metrics by customer segment (Enterprise, Mid-Market, SMB).

## Output (B2B PMF Scorecard)
Must include:
1.  **5 Core Metrics**: Logo Retention, NRR, TTV, Champion Risk, Expansion Rate.
2.  **Segment Breakdown**: Metrics by customer tier to identify where PMF is strong/weak.
3.  **Risk Flags**: Explicit callouts of champion turnover or segment underperformance.
4.  **Verdict**: Clear "Strong/Moderate/Weak PMF" assessment with rationale.

# Strategy Refresh Chain

## Goal
To update the strategic direction and align the team.

## Steps
1.  **Review**: Call `OKR Guardian`. Get `Q3 Scorecard`.
2.  **Update**: Call `Portfolio Orchestrator`. Generate `Q4 Roadmap Candidate`.
3.  **Gate**: **[STOP]** Ask for Roadmap Approval.
4.  **Alignment**: Call `Session Facilitator`. Schedule `Alignment Workshop`.

## Output (State Tracking)
- **Status**: `IN_PROGRESS` | `BLOCKED` | `DONE`
- **Current Step**: [Step Name]
- **Artifacts**: [Link to Scorecard], [Link to Roadmap]

## Audition
"Q3 Strategy Refresh complete. OKR Score: 7/10. Q4 Roadmap drafted with 3 key initiatives. Workshop agenda prepared. Ready for review."
